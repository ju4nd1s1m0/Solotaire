How to play

Build the four foundations up from Ace to King, one for each suit.

On the table, stack cards down in alternating colors. Move a run by dragging its top card. Only a King can fill an empty column.

Tap a card to send it to the best spot automatically. Drag to place it yourself.

Tap the deck to turn cards. When it's empty, tap the outline to recycle.

Use Hint when stuck, and Auto-finish appears once the path home is clear.

Hold a card for two seconds to light up everywhere it can legally go.
